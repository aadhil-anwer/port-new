About

My Story

I'm Aadhil Anwar — a security engineer who started out curious about how systems break and turned that into a career making sure they don't.

Currently working in network security at GoDaddy, I spend my days thinking about vulnerability research, API security, network defense, and offensive testing. Previously, I was a Network Engineering Intern at LPU, where I automated log analysis, hunted network anomalies, and hardened infrastructure.

I've reported high-severity CVEs in production APIs, led cybersecurity workshops for 2000+ students, and built servers from raw TCP sockets because I wanted to understand what's actually happening at the wire level.
